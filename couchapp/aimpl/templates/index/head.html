The AIM Problem Lists are part of the Bibliographic Knowledge Network (BKN) project funded by a Cyber Enabled Discovery and Innovation (CDI) grant from the National Science Foundation. Major partners in the project are UC Berkeley, Harvard, Stanford, and AIM.

Four sample problem lists went online on November 18, 2009. The lists are open to contributions by anyone, with oversight by experts in the subject of each problem list. More lists, with more features, are planned for the near future.

AIM Problem List content is licensed under a Creative Commons, Attribution NoDerivative Works license. The right licensing structure for AIM Problem lists is a subject of active discussion currently, and we invite interested parties to contribute to the discussion and give feedback.

Questions or comments to problemlists@aimath.org
